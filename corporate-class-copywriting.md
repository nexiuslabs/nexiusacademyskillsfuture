Decision support

**Headline**

When a private run makes more sense than a public class

**Body**

Public intakes work when individual staff want exposure. A dedicated company run works better when relevance, privacy, internal alignment, and implementation follow-through matter more.

### Comparison

| Decision area | Public class | Dedicated company class |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Examples used in class | General examples designed for mixed learners | Can be anchored around your own business processes |
| Room to discuss internal workflows | Usually limited because the cohort is mixed | Stronger because the discussion stays within your own company setting |
| Schedule flexibility | Fixed to public intake dates | Planned around your team availability and operations |
| Adoption after training | Often depends on individuals driving change back internally | Easier to align on next steps across the full team at once |

## FAQ

**Section label**

FAQ

**Headline**

Answers to common questions

**Body**

Answers to common questions about dedicated company runs.

### Who is this private class for?

It is for companies that want to train one internal team or multiple departments together in a dedicated cohort instead of joining a public class.

### What is the minimum class size?

The minimum class size is 12 pax for a dedicated company run.

### Can the workshop focus on our real internal workflows?

Yes. The company-run format is specifically designed to make the workshop more relevant to your team's actual use cases, recurring tasks, and process pain points.

### Can you run the training at our office?

Yes. We can discuss delivery at your own office or at an external venue depending on privacy, convenience, and room setup.

### Is this suitable for non-technical teams?

Yes. The course is designed for non-technical professionals and focuses on practical workflow use, not coding.

### How is pricing handled for a company class?

We prepare a custom quotation based on your team size, learner profile, venue preference, and scheduling requirements.

### Can leaders and frontline staff attend together?

Yes. In many cases that works well because it helps the organisation align on use cases, governance, and realistic implementation steps.

### What if we have fewer than 12 pax?

You can still contact us. We can advise whether a public intake, a later internal run, or a combined team option makes more sense.
