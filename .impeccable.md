## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are company decision-makers, team leads, and business-function managers evaluating AI training for internal teams. They are comparing formats quickly and want to understand relevance, practicality, governance fit, and next-step feasibility without reading something that feels overly technical.

### Brand Personality
Credible, practical, refined. The interface should feel confident and business-ready, with enough editorial polish to feel premium but never flashy or trend-chasing.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the existing Nexius Academy site palette and stay visually consistent with the rest of the site. Prefer a light, editorial, structured layout over startup-style dark gradients or generic SaaS card stacks. The page should feel clearer, more intentional, and more balanced rather than louder.

### Design Principles
1. Make business clarity obvious within a few seconds through hierarchy and spacing.
2. Preserve the site's established color language and professional tone.
3. Use asymmetry and varied density to create rhythm, but keep the overall reading flow simple.
4. Group related content into clear decision-making blocks rather than long stacked columns.
5. Prefer composed layouts that feel designed over repetitive card piles.
